Our Services

Career Counselling

Our first service, Career Counselling, is dedicated to assisting individuals in various stages of their professional journey. This includes fresh graduates, early-career job seekers, those aiming for a career change, individuals re-entering the job market, and anyone aspiring to advance their careers globally. Our comprehensive approach aims to guide and support clients in their career development endeavors. We offer a range of personalized services, such as collaboratively defining career goals, devising effective job search strategies, facilitating career transitions, refining resumes and cover letters, creating actionable job search plans, fostering career progression, providing insights into promotions and salary negotiations, delivering interview preparation tailored to the tech/software industry, and imparting office and work ethic guidance for the first month on the job. Our offerings are backed by a wealth of diverse industry experience spanning various sectors and domains, contributing to a robust job search strategy. With a track record of over 80 successful interviews, a growing clientele of 37 individuals and counting, an impressive average salary increase of 32% among our clients, and an exceptional success rate exceeding 95%, we take pride in our ability to shape and define your career guidance strategy effectively. Let us help you pave the way towards a fulfilling and prosperous professional future.

Resume Writing

Our second service, which focuses on Resume Writing, entails providing comprehensive and professional editing and writing services for ATS-optimized resumes and curriculum vitae (CVs). With extensive experience in assisting clients across various industries and countries, I deliver a customized and unparalleled service that merges high-quality content with exceptional design. Recognizing the competitiveness of today's job market, I understand the importance of tailoring services to individual needs. As part of this commitment, I offer unlimited guidance, advice, and support throughout the entire process to ensure that the service you receive aligns perfectly with your requirements. Additionally, I excel in crafting cover letters that set my diverse clientele apart and help them establish a strong presence in the market. Drawing from a solid academic and professional background, I am skilled in adapting my writing style to highlight the unique strengths of each client.
